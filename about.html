About Us

Mr. Chetan Kharatmal, the visionary behind CBK Groups and Cyber Tech, is a dynamic entrepreneur known for his pioneering spirit and innovative contributions in the tech industry. With a passion for technology and a drive to revolutionize the digital landscape, he embarked on a journey to create cutting-edge solutions that redefine the way we interact with the online world. As a tech enthusiast from a young age, Mr. Kharatmal displayed an exceptional aptitude for coding and development. His early experiences and insatiable curiosity led him to establish CBK Groups, a conglomerate dedicated to spearheading technological advancements across various domains. Under his leadership, CBK Groups has emerged as a multifaceted entity, encompassing diverse ventures spanning software development, IT consultancy, and digital solutions. Cyber Tech, a flagship initiative under the CBK Groups umbrella, stands as a testament to Mr. Kharatmal's vision. This division focuses on cybersecurity, leveraging state-of-the-art techniques to safeguard digital infrastructures against evolving threats. Through Cyber Tech, Mr. Kharatmal has been instrumental in fortifying online ecosystems, ensuring robust protection for businesses and individuals alike. His commitment to innovation extends beyond conventional boundaries. The creation of the voice-controlled calculator webpage exemplifies his endeavor to blend convenience with technological prowess. By integrating speech recognition technology into a user-friendly calculator interface, he aims to simplify daily tasks and enhance accessibility for diverse user demographics. Mr. Kharatmal's leadership style resonates with empowerment and inclusivity. He fosters a collaborative work culture within CBK Groups, encouraging creativity and fostering a conducive environment for talent to thrive. His team-driven approach has been pivotal in nurturing groundbreaking ideas and bringing them to fruition. Beyond his entrepreneurial pursuits, Mr. Chetan Kharatmal is recognized as a mentor and influencer in the tech community. He actively engages in knowledge-sharing initiatives, mentoring aspiring entrepreneurs, and advocating for technological literacy.
